Concession Stand

Directions

Your school's booster club runs a concession stand at sporting events to raise money. They currently sale the following items:
Item Cost
Hamburger 2.00
Hotdog 1.50
Chips .50
Candy 1.00
Drink 1.00

They have asked you to write a program that will allow them to enter the num of items sold for each food type and then generate a receipt showing the cost of each food type along with a grand total of the cost of the food. They want the receipt to only show food items that were purchased.
