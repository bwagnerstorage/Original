Rescue Princess

Directions

An evil witch has kidnapped a princess and has taken her to the witch's castle. Her castle is surrounded by three impassable moats. The king has dispatched his most skilled and courageous knight to find the princess. Unknown to the witch, trolls loyal to the king have built a secret bridge across each of the three moats.

Write a program that will allow the knight to find the secret bridges, enter the castle, and find the princess.

knight: starting location (0, 0)
princess: starting location (9, 14)
