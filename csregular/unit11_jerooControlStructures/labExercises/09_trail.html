Trail

Directions

Jose has two trails through a forest of flowers to his favorite lake. Unfortunately Brian discovered his trails and keeps setting traps all along their path. Write a program that will allow Jose to follow his trails to his favorite lake by disabling all the nets. You should by able to place Jose at the entrance to either trail and he should be able to find his way to the lake.

Entrance to Trail 1: (0,1)
Entrance to Trail 2: (0,22)
