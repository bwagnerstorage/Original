Gardening

Directions

A jeroo loves flowers so much that he wants to cover the entire island with them. He decides the best way to plant the flowers is in a spiral pattern where he starts on the perimeter of the island and continues to move toward the center until the entire island is covered with flowers. Write a program that allows the jeroo to plant flowers in this spiral pattern. The jeroo should stop when the entire island is covered with flowers.
