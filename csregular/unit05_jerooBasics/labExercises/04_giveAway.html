Give Away

Directions

Three Jeroos (you give them names) are practicing for the Santong Relays. The figure below shows the course at the start of the race. The first Jeroo begins at (4,9) facing EAST. It must pick the flower and give it to the second Jeroo who begins at (7,13) facing NORTH. The second gives the flower to the third Jeroo who begins at (4,15) facing WEST. The third Jeroo must plant the flower at (3,16), run to (3,21) and stop.
