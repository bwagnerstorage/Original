Directions

In this assignment you are going to write a program that calculates a person's weight on different planets according each planet's force of gravity. The force of gravity is different for each of the nine planets in our solar system. For example, on Mercury it is only 0.38 times as strong as on Earth. The table below shows the force of gravity on each planet relative to the force of gravity on Earth.
