Relay Race

Directions

Create 4 Jeroos, one in each corner of RelayRaceIsland as pictured below. Each Jeroo starts out holding a single flower. The first Jeroo is standing on top of a flower which it picks immediately and then races forward to the next Jeroo (disabling the net along the way, of course) and passes the flower on. The second Jeroo passes the flower to the third, and the third to the fourth. There should be one well-constructed method that all 4 Jeroos can use to complete the race.

Reminder: To pass a flower from one Jeroo to the next use the give method in one of the following forms.

give(AHEAD);
give(RIGHT);
give(LEFT);
