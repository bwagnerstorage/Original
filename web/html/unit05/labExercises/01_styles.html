Lab Exercise

  1. Open Notepad and create a new document named 5_1_styles.html and save it to your HTML folder.

  2. Give the document the title: 5_1_styles.

  3. Create a html document that includes the following:

    • The centered heading My Favorite Sport/Hobbie.

    • A paragraph that lists your favorite sport or hobbie and explains why it is your favorite.

    • Change the background color of the page to Azure.

    • Change the text color of the paragraph to DarkGreen.

    • Change the font of the paragraph to Calibri. An element can have more than one style property. Simply insert a semicolon between them.

      style="property:value; property:value"
