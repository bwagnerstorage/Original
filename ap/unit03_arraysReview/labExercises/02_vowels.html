Vowels

Directions

Write a program that allows a user to enter 10 words from the keyboard. As the words are entered store them in an array of Strings. After the array has been populated with strings, count the number of vowels contained in each word and display the results like the sample output below.

Decompose the problem into appropriate methods.

Source File

Vowels.java

Sample Run

Enter 10 words:
Word 1-->elephant
Word 2-->dog
Word 3-->horse
Word 4-->camel
Word 5-->chicken
Word 6-->sheep
Word 7-->cow
Word 8-->rooster
Word 9-->lamb
Word 10-->calf

elephant - 3
dog - 1
horse - 2
camel - 2
chicken - 2
sheep - 2
cow - 1
rooster - 3
lamb - 1
calf - 1
