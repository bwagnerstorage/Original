School Dance

Directions

The student council at your school is sponsoring a dance and they have asked you to write a program to help them track the number of students entering the dance and how much money is made off of ticket sales. The council has decided to sell tickets to the dance according to the Renaissance incentives program. (Renaissance is the school's rewards program that offers discounts to school functions for students who make good grades.)

Ticket prices are as follows:

Renaissance Level Ticket Price

  • Gold - Free
  • Silver - 2.00
  • Bronze - 4.00
  • No Pass - 6.00

A student holding a gold card is admitted into the dance at no charge. A student holding a silver card is charged 2.00 dollars, a student holding a bronze card is charged 4.00 dollars, and a student without a Renaissance card is charged 6.00 dollars.
