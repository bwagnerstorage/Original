Lab Exercise

Write a Python program that calculates a student's Semester Average given as input the two 9 Week Averages and the Semester Exam grade.

The formula for calculating the Semester Average is

Semester Average = Average of two 9 Weeks times 80% plus the Semester Exam times 20%

The program's output should look like the Sample Run below.

Use Python's built-in function round to calculate the Semester Average rounded to an integer.

Sample Run

Enter 1st 9 weeks grade -->85
Enter 2nd 9 weeks grade -->97
Enter Semester Exam grade -->95

  Calculate Semester Average
-------------------------------
1st 9 Week Average  = 85
2nd 9 Week Average  = 97
Semester Exam Grade = 95
-------------------------------
Semester Average    = 91.8
Rounded Average     = 92
