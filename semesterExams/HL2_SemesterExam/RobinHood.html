Robin Hood is a heroic outlaw in English folklore who, according to legend, was a highly skilled archer and swordsman. He is often portrayed as "robbing from the rich and giving to the poor" alongside his band of Merry Men.

A town is represented in the RobinHood class as a 2D array of of houses. Each house contains a family that is either poor or wealthy. A family is poor if their wealth is less than or equal to 10 and a family is wealthy if their wealth is greater than 10. Look at the following example of a 3 by 3 2D array that represents a town.
