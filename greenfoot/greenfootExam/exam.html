Greenfoot Exam

Download and Open Scenario

  1. Click on the following link to download the scenario: Greenfoot Exam

  2. Select Open.

  3. Copy the folder GreenfootExam and paste it in your myGreenfoot folder.

  4. Launch Greenfoot and select Open from the Scenario menu.

  5. Navigate to your MyGreenfoot folder, select the Greenfoot Exam scenario, and click Open.


Question 1 - Pig

Modify the Pig class's act method so that a pig will move across the world from left to right when the Run button is pressed.

Question 2 - Wombat

Modify the Wombat class's act method so that a wombat will move down the world from top to bottom when the Run button is pressed.

Question 3 - TeddyBear

Modify the TeddyBear class's act method so that a teddybear will move to the left when the left arrow key is pressed and move to the right when the right arrow key is pressed.

Question 4 - Car

Modify the Car class's act method so that a car will move horizontally between two walls when the Run button is pressed. When the car touches a wall it needs to change its direction and move toward the other wall (i.e. "bounce" off the wall).

Question 5 - Penguin

Modify the Penguin class's act method so that a penguin will rotate 90 degrees each time the left mouse button is clicked on the penguin.

Hints: Use the keyword "this" when the Peguin class needs to refer to itself.
